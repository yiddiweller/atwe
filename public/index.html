Privacy Policy & Terms

Before creating your Atwe account, please review how we handle your information and the rules for using Atwe.

What we collect. Your name, email, username, password (stored only as a secure hash), optional photo and date of birth, the content you post, your messages, the AI conversations you have, and basic usage data.

How we use it. To run the Service, power AI replies, messaging, calls and live streams, secure your account, send account emails, and prevent abuse.

Who we share with. Only trusted providers that host our servers, deliver email, process payments, power AI responses, and relay calls and live streams. We never sell your data.

Your content & control. Your public posts are visible to others; direct messages stay private to their participants. You choose who can contact and call you, you can block anyone, and you can delete your full history at any time.

The rules. You must be 18 or older, keep your account secure, only post content you have the right to share, and never harass, impersonate, spam, or break the law. We may remove content or suspend accounts that break these rules.

AI. AI responses are generated automatically and may be inaccurate — don't rely on them as professional advice.
